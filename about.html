About Cedar Valley Nursery

Cedar Valley Nursery is a 3rd generation family farm located in the rural parts of beautiful Hudson, Wisconsin. We serve Western Wisconsin and the surrounding communities. Our family's 17 acre tree farm was created through two decades of planting a variety of trees in order to create screens, add fall colors, and attract wildlife on our farm. We soon ran out of planting space and had to begin selling trees to make room to grow yours.

Cedar Valley Nursery has been growing trees since 1992 and selling since 2004. To better serve our customers, our nursery has since invested in a 44 inch tree spade. We are now able to offer installation of trees ranging in size from 7 feet to up to 18 feet with up to 5 inch diameter trunk. While Cedar Valley Nursery continues to grow trees, we are happy to also offer more services to include installing potted trees, shrubs and perennials as well as basic landscaping and yard work.

Cedar Valley Nursery has always prided itself on providing strong customer service. We enjoy working one on one with you on tree placement design and to answer any questions you may have pertaining to the quality and care of your yard. Cedar Valley is here to help you in any way so you can begin to create that ideal yard you have always been striving to develop.
