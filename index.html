Warranty

Customer satisfaction is very important to Cedar Valley Nursery. We will warranty all trees, plants and shrubs for one year from the date of planting. If after a determination that a new tree or plant installation is required, a moving and/or installation cost will apply.

Hours of Operation

Cedar Valley Nursery offers flexible hours seven days a week. Please contact us to make an appointment to tour our farm. Enjoy a ride on our three passenger Polaris while picking out your trees and enjoying the beautiful country scenery. We are also available to come to your home and offer free consultation and advice on any landscaping questions you may have concerning your trees and plants.
