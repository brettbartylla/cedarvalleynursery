Cemetery Services

Planting Urn and delivering to the cemetery before Memorial Day Flower arrangement includes 3 Geraniums, 1 spike or fern, vina vines. Stands and Urns are available for purchase. Weekly watering and monthly fertilizing plus winter storing of urn and stand. We currently service Saint Patrick Cemetery, Willow River Cemetery and Saint Bridgets Cemetery.

Tree Spading

We can transplant a wide variety of trees from our farm to your residence. The trees range from 7ft -14 feet tall and up to 5-6 inches in diameter depending upon species.

Landscaping

We offer a wide variety of trees shade, ornamental and evergreen trees to create your landscape. We also offer potted trees, small gardens, shrubs and perennials. If we don't have the tree, shrub or perennial on the farm, chances are we can locate and provide what you need to finish your landscaping project.

Basic Landscaping

Install gardens, yard clean-up, mulching, tree trimming and shrub pruning. Deliver black dirt and mulch.
